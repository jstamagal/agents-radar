### 3. Trend Signal Analysis

**1. The "Claude Code" Effect & Agentic Harnessing**
The most significant signal today is the community's response to **Claude Code**. We are seeing an explosion of "meta-projects"—tools that wrap, configure, or harness AI coding models.
*   **Harnesses & Skills:** Projects like *Archon*, *Superpowers*, and *andrej-karpathy-skills* indicate that developers are no longer satisfied with just "chatting with AI." They want **standardized, repeatable workflows**. The focus is shifting from prompt engineering to **framework engineering**.
*   **Instillation:** The popularity of configuration files (like the Karpathy skills) suggests that "instilling" high-level coding behaviors into agents is becoming a valuable skill set.

**2. Verticalization: Finance & SEO**
The days of generic "do everything" agents are yielding to specialized vertical solutions.
*   **Kronos (Finance)** and **SEOMachine (Marketing)** prove that the market is moving toward **high-value domain agents**. These aren't just toys; they are designed to replace expensive human labor in specific sectors (Financial Analysts, SEO Writers).

**3. The Data Bottleneck: PDFs & Audio**
As LLMs become smarter, the bottleneck is shifting to high-quality input.
*   **opendataloader-pdf** highlights that simply feeding PDFs to LLMs is insufficient; complex, structured cleaning is required.
*   **VoxCPM** (Tokenizer-Free) suggests that the next frontier of LLM evolution is **efficiency in non-text modalities**, removing the rigid tokenizer bottleneck for more natural speech synthesis.
